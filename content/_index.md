Dr. Mengtao Lyu is currently a Postdoctoral researcher at the [Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/aae/), [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/). He received his PhD in Human-Computer Interactions from the same department, MSc in Supply Chain & Lgistics from [School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/mae), [Nanyang Technological University (NTU)](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/), and BEng in Software Engineering and BA in Japanese from [School of Software](https://ss.dlut.edu.cn/), [Dalian University of Technology (DUT)](https://www.dlut.edu.cn/).

Mengtao is a member of [The Hong Kong Ergonomics Society](https://www.ergonomics.org.hk/), and a member of [Chinese Ergonomics Society](http://www.cesbj.org/). He serves as a reviewer for many international journals and conferences, including: [<em>Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence</em>](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/engineering-applications-of-artificial-intelligence), [<em>Advanced Engineering Informatics</em>](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/advanced-engineering-informatics), [<em>Knowledge-Based Systems</em>](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/knowledge-based-systems),[<em> Computers & Industrial Engineering</em>](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/computers-and-industrial-engineering), [<em>IEEE Transactions on Human-Machine Systems</em>](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/xpl/RecentIssue.jsp?punumber=6221037), [<em>International Journal of Human-Computer Interaction</em>](https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/hihc20), [<em>Frontiers in Neuroscience</em>](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/neuroscience).

In addition to the academic activities, Mengtao used to work as an IT consultant at [ABeam Consulting](https://www.abeam.com/jp/en/), and as an IT supervisor at [Asics Corp](https://corp.asics.com/).


## Research Interest
