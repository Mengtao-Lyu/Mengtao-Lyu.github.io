Dr. Mengtao Lyu is currently a Postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering , The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) . He received his PhD in Human-Computer Interactions from the same department, MSc in Supply Chain & Lgistics from School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering , Nanyang Technological University (NTU) , and BEng in Software Engineering and BA in Japanese from School of Software , Dalian University of Technology (DUT) .

Mengtao is a member of The Hong Kong Ergonomics Society , and a member of Chinese Ergonomics Society . He serves as a reviewer for many international journals and conferences, including: Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence , Advanced Engineering Informatics , Knowledge-Based Systems , Computers & Industrial Engineering , IEEE Transactions on Human-Machine Systems , International Journal of Human-Computer Interaction , Frontiers in Neuroscience .

In addition to the academic activities, Mengtao used to work as an IT consultant at ABeam Consulting , and as an IT supervisor at Asics Corp .
