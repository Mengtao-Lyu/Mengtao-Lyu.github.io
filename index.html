👋 Welcome, I’m Mengtao Lyu (Tomy) 😁
Ph.D. | Postdoctoral researcher
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University

Mengtao Lyu is currently a Postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering , The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) . He received his PhD in Human-Computer Interactions from the same department, MSc in Supply Chain & Lgistics from School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering , Nanyang Technological University (NTU) , and BEng in Software Engineering from School of Software , Dalian University of Technology (DUT)

Mengtao is a member of The Hong Kong Ergonomics Society , and a member of Chinese Ergonomics Society .

Mengtao used to work as an IT consultant at ABeam Consulting , and as an IT supervisor at Asics Corp .
